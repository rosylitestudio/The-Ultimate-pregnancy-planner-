Postpartum Wellness Tips

💙 Week 1-2: Focus on rest, hydration, and pain management. Let others help with household tasks.
💚 Week 3-6: Gentle walks, light stretching. Avoid strenuous activity. Continue monitoring bleeding.
💛 Week 6+: Return to doctor for clearance before resuming exercise. Ease back gradually.
